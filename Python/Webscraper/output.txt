</p><p><a href="/Penny" title="Penny">Penny</a> tutors <a href="/Vincent" title="Vincent">Vincent</a> and <a href="/Jas" title="Jas">Jas</a> in the library on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Friday.
</p>

<p>There are 2 <a href="/Achievements" title="Achievements">Achievements</a> associated with donating <a href="/Artifacts" title="Artifacts">Artifacts</a> or <a href="/Minerals" title="Minerals">Minerals</a> to the Museum.
</p>
<ul><li>Treasure Trove (Donate 40 different items to the museum)</li>
<li>A Complete Collection (Complete the museum collection)</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span></h2>
<ul><li>There is a total of 102 spaces available for donations to be placed but a total of only 95 available items to be donated. Six spaces in the center of the largest table cannot be reached to examine items placed on them, so it may be advisable to leave this area empty.
<ul><li>If using a controller, any diagonal item may be unreachable for examination once donated.</li></ul></li>
<li>In order to move items inside the donation interface on the Switch, you'll have to go into "Options" and disable "Use Controller Style Menus".</li></ul>
